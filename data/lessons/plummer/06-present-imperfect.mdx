## Present Indicative — All Voices

**Active endings** — subject performs the action:

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | -ω | -ομεν |
| 2nd | -εις | -ετε |
| 3rd | -ει | -ουσι(ν) |

**Middle/Passive endings** — subject receives or acts with personal interest:

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | -ομαι | -όμεθα |
| 2nd | -ῃ | -εσθε |
| 3rd | -εται | -ονται |

## The Imperfect Indicative

The imperfect expresses **continuous action in past time**. Formed with:
1. **Augment** (ε- prefix for consonant-initial verbs; vowel lengthening for vowel-initial)
2. **Present tense stem**
3. **Secondary endings** (slightly different from primary)

**Secondary Active Endings:**

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | -ον | -ομεν |
| 2nd | -ες | -ετε |
| 3rd | -ε(ν) | -ον |

**ἔλυον** = "I was loosing" (imperfect active indicative 1sg of λύω)
