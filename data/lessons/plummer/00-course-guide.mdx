## About This Series

*Beginning with New Testament Greek* (BNTG) by Benjamin L. Merkle and Robert L. Plummer is one of the most student-friendly and pedagogically current elementary Greek grammars available. It is designed for both one-semester and two-semester courses, incorporates current scholarship on verbal aspect and middle voice, and provides extensive free digital resources through the publisher and through Daily Dose of Greek.

This lesson series embeds Dr. Plummer's chapter overview video for each of the 24 chapters directly into each lesson page, alongside original grammar content, vocabulary, and practice exercises.

**How to use these lessons:**

1. Watch the embedded video lecture (10–25 minutes each).
2. Read the lesson's grammar summary and vocabulary table.
3. Complete the practice exercises in our app — your progress is tracked automatically.
4. Use the Additional Resources at the bottom of each lesson for further study.
