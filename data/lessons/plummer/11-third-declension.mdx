## The Third Declension

Third declension nouns have **variable stems** — the stem is revealed by the **genitive singular** (remove -ος). You must learn the genitive of each third declension noun as part of its vocabulary entry.

**Stem = genitive singular − ος**

### Masculine/Feminine Endings

| Case | Sg. | Pl. |
|------|-----|-----|
| Nom. | *varies* | -ες |
| Gen. | -ος | -ων |
| Dat. | -ι | -σι(ν) |
| Acc. | -α | -ας |

### Neuter Endings

| Case | Sg. | Pl. |
|------|-----|-----|
| Nom. | *varies* | -α |
| Gen. | -ος | -ων |
| Dat. | -ι | -σι(ν) |
| Acc. | = nom. | -α |

### Key Paradigms

**σάρξ, σαρκός, ἡ** (*flesh* — mute stem σαρκ-)

| Nom. Sg. | Gen. Sg. | Dat. Sg. | Acc. Sg. |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| σάρξ | σαρκός | σαρκί | σάρκα |

**πνεῦμα, πνεύματος, τό** (*spirit* — neuter -ματ- stem)

| Nom. Sg. | Gen. Sg. | Dat. Sg. | Acc. Sg. |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| πνεῦμα | πνεύματος | πνεύματι | πνεῦμα |
