## μι Verbs

Most Greek verbs are **ω verbs**. A smaller but crucial class are **μι verbs** — they use different endings and form tenses differently in the present and aorist.

The four essential μι verbs in the NT:

| Verb | Meaning | NT Freq. |
|------|---------|----------|
| δίδωμι | I give | 415 |
| τίθημι | I place, put | 100 |
| ἵστημι | I stand / cause to stand | 154 |
| ἀφίημι | I forgive, release, permit | 143 |

## Present Active Indicative — δίδωμι

μι verbs **reduplicate the stem** in the present tense (δι-δω-) and attach endings **without a connecting vowel** in the singular:

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | δίδω**μι** | δίδο**μεν** |
| 2nd | δίδω**ς** | δίδο**τε** |
| 3rd | δίδω**σι(ν)** | δίδό**ασι(ν)** |

## Aorist Active — ἔδωκα (*I gave*)

δίδωμι uses a **κ aorist** in some forms:

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | ἔδωκα | ἔδομεν |
| 2nd | ἔδωκας | ἔδοτε |
| 3rd | ἔδωκε(ν) | ἔδοσαν |

## οἶδα — *I Know*

**οἶδα** is a **perfect form with present meaning** — "I know" (not "I have known"). It is extremely common (318 occurrences).

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | οἶδα | οἴδαμεν |
| 2nd | οἶδας | οἴδατε |
| 3rd | οἶδε(ν) | οἴδασι(ν) |

Pluperfect (= imperfect meaning): ᾔδειν (*I knew*)

## Key Imperative Forms

| Form | Verb | Meaning |
|------|------|---------|
| δός | δίδωμι | Give! |
| θές | τίθημι | Put! |
| ἄφες | ἀφίημι | Forgive! |
| στῆθι | ἵστημι | Stand! |
