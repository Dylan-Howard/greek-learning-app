## Aorist Passive Indicative

The aorist passive uses the tense formative **θη** (or η in some verbs):

**Formula:** augment + stem + **θη** + secondary **active** endings

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | ἐλύ**θην** | ἐλύ**θημεν** |
| 2nd | ἐλύ**θης** | ἐλύ**θητε** |
| 3rd | ἐλύ**θη** | ἐλύ**θησαν** |

> Note: The aorist passive uses **active** endings — not middle. This is unique.

## Future Passive Indicative

Built on the aorist passive stem + primary **middle** endings:

**Formula:** stem + **θησ** + primary middle/passive endings

| Person | Sg. | Pl. |
|--------|-----|-----|
| 1st | λυ**θήσομαι** | λυ**θησόμεθα** |
| 2nd | λυ**θήσῃ** | λυ**θήσεσθε** |
| 3rd | λυ**θήσεται** | λυ**θήσονται** |

## Second Aorist Passive

Some verbs form the aorist passive without θ, using just **η** directly:

- γράφω → ἐγράφην (*I was written*)
- ἀποστέλλω → ἀπεστάλην (*I was sent*)

These must be memorized when encountered.
