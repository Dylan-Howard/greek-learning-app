## About This Series

William Mounce's *Basics of Biblical Greek* (BBG) is the most widely used seminary-level introduction to New Testament Greek in the English-speaking world. This lesson series follows the 4th edition chapter-by-chapter, presenting the grammar and vocabulary in each chapter alongside curated practice, and linking you to Mounce's own lectures at BiblicalTraining.org where he works through every concept in detail.

**How to use these lessons:**

1. Read the lesson page here to get oriented.
2. Click **"Watch the Lecture"** to open Mounce's lecture on BiblicalTraining.org.
3. Return here to complete the vocabulary drills and grammar exercises in our app.
4. Your progress is tracked automatically when you complete each lesson's practice module.
