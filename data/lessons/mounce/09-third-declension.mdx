## What Makes the Third Declension Different

First and second declension nouns have relatively uniform stems and endings. Third declension nouns have **variable stems** — the stem is not always visible from the nominative singular. You must learn the **genitive singular** of every third declension noun to find its true stem.

**The stem = genitive singular minus the genitive singular ending (-ος)**

Example: σάρξ (*flesh*) — genitive singular is **σαρκ**ός → stem = **σαρκ-**

## Third Declension Endings

### Masculine/Feminine Third Declension

| Case | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nominative | varies | -ες |
| Genitive | -ος | -ων |
| Dative | -ι | -σι(ν) |
| Accusative | -α or -ν | -ας |

> The **dative plural** ending **-σι(ν)** sometimes causes changes to the stem's final consonant (e.g., σαρξίν from σαρκ-).

### Neuter Third Declension

| Case | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nominative | varies | -α |
| Genitive | -ος | -ων |
| Dative | -ι | -σι(ν) |
| Accusative | = nominative | -α |

Neuter rule holds: **nominative = accusative** in both singular and plural.

## Sample Paradigms

### σάρξ, σαρκός, ἡ (*flesh, body*) — mute stem

| Case | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nominative | σάρξ | σάρκ**ες** |
| Genitive | σαρκ**ός** | σαρκ**ῶν** |
| Dative | σαρκ**ί** | σαρξ**ίν** |
| Accusative | σάρκ**α** | σάρκ**ας** |

> Note: σαρκ + σιν → σαρξίν (κ+σ = ξ)

### πνεῦμα, πνεύματος, τό (*spirit, wind*) — neuter -ματ- stem

| Case | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nominative | πνεῦμα | πνεύματ**α** |
| Genitive | πνεύματ**ος** | πνευμάτ**ων** |
| Dative | πνεύματ**ι** | πνεύμα**σι(ν)** |
| Accusative | πνεῦμα | πνεύματ**α** |

> The -ματ- stem drops the τ in the nominative/accusative singular and the dative plural.

### χάρις, χάριτος, ἡ (*grace*) — -ιτ- stem

| Case | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nominative | χάρις | χάριτ**ες** |
| Genitive | χάριτ**ος** | χαρίτ**ων** |
| Dative | χάριτ**ι** | χάρι**σι(ν)** |
| Accusative | χάριν | χάριτ**ας** |
